​ We have been outfitting the Chester County area with fishing tackle for over 40 years now. We always are in search for new products coming out and trying to stay on top of the latest fishing tackle trends. As always we are also stocked up on the old standby lures of the past. With our vast selection of tackle we are sure to have you covered for just about any type of freshwater fishing, be it for bass, trout, musky, catfish or even panfish (bluegill, perch or crappie) We have an extensive inventory of rods, reels, lures and accessories from manufacturers listed below and more. We also carry live bait year round! Worms, night crawlers, wax worms, meal worms, fathead minnows, and Arkansas shiners.
