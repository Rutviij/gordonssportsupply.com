Firearms

We love firearms as much as you do, our staff is always willing to help you with any of your shooting needs. We have everything from hunting rifles, shotguns, handguns to guns for sport and target shooting. Everyone at the shop is a firearms owner and very knowledgeable when it comes to the products we sell. In addition to new guns we have one of the largest selections of pre-owned firearms in the area. We buy single guns as well as collections. So if you are looking to Buy Sell or Trade, we are the shop for you. If you are new to owning a firearm or just need advice and or recommendations on what to buy, we can certainly offer guidance to help you make the most sensible and practical choice for your needs. ​ We also offer gunsmithing services at our location. We can handle most of your needs from general repairs, parts installs, trigger installation, scope installations, sight installs, cleanings, drilling and tapping etc. We work on almost all types and models of firearms. AR-15 modification and parts installs. AK sight work (canted sight fixing) If we can't fix it we can tell you who can. ​ We offer new and used firearms from most major manufacturers. Our inventory is constantly changing so please check back often to see what is new. We also keep a used firearms list on the website.
